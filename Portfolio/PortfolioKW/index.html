About me

My name is Katharine Wilson and I’m an aspiring political reporter studying journalism and politics at The University of Maryland, College Park.

As a staff writer for The Diamondback, I’ve covered The College Park City Council, city development, protests and led coverage for a mayoral election. This semester I am also a breaking news reporting intern for Montgomery Community Media and a producer for WMUC-FM Radio.

Last summer, I was an editorial intern for The Herald-Mail where I wrote feature and breaking news stories. I have also interned for College Park Here & Now as a city council reporter and for News Not Noise as a social media and research intern.

After graduation, I plan to use my experiences to humanize national politics through feature stories, hold governmental officials to account through investigations and help people understand and improve their own community.

Please feel free to reach out with any news tips, opportunities or questions.
