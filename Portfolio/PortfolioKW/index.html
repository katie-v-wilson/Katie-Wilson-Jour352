About me

Hello my name is Katharine Wilson. Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a junior journalism and politics student at the University of Maryland, College Park.

Through multiple internships and jobs with student media organizations, I have covered federal, county and city politics. I have also reported on a wide range of news topics including businesses, education, art, the environment, and the criminal justice system.

This semester I am covering the federal government as a Washington D.C. bureau reporter for Capital News Service. I am also returning to Montgomery Community Media as a freelance reporter, The Diamondback as the city and campus development reporter, and WMUC-FM Radio as an entertainment news producer.

After graduating in May 2025, I plan spend my reporting career writing stories that humanize political issues, hold people in power accountable and help people understand their community.
