About me

My name is Katharine Wilson and I’m a journalism and politics student at The University of Maryland, College Park. I have experience covering local, state and national politics for a variety of news organizations across Maryland.

As a staff writer for The Diamondback, I’ve covered The College Park City Council, city development, protests, and elections. I am a breaking news reporting intern for Montgomery Community Media and a producer for WMUC-FM Radio.

Last summer I was an editorial intern for The Herald-Mail where I wrote features and breaking news stories. I have previously interned for College Park Here & Now as a city council reporter and for News Not Noise as a social media and research intern.

I will be reporting for the Washington D.C. bureau of Capital News Service in spring 2024.

After graduation, I plan to spend my journalism careeer humanizing national politics through feature stories, holding governmental officials accountable through investigations and helping people understand and improve their community.

Please feel free to reach out with any news tips, opportunities, or questions.
